About the Hackathon

Design AI-Powered Policing for India

The Delhi Police Hackathon – Manthan 2025 brings together students, researchers, industry professionals, and innovators to design AI-powered solutions that redefine the future of policing in India. From indigenous CCTV hardware to cyber defense frameworks, Manthan challenges teams to build secure, scalable, and cost-effective innovations ready for deployment.

This platform bridges the gap between technology creators and policing needs, ensuring India’s law enforcement is future-ready to combat emerging digital and physical threats.

Participation Details

Who Can Apply

Participation Requirements
  • Team participation only (2–6 members); individual entries are not accepted.
  • Open to students, PhD scholars, researchers, and industry professionals.
  • Teams must be nominated via academic institutions or industry partners.
  • Nomination package must include team credentials, institution details, and proposed area of innovation.
Judging Criteria

How We Evaluate

Evaluation Framework
  • Innovation & Creativity – originality and disruptive thinking.
  • Technical Implementation – feasibility, scalability, and robustness.
  • Depth & Technical Detailing – clear workflows, diagrams, and frameworks.
  • Impact on Security – strengthening surveillance and cyber resilience.
  • Presentation & Clarity – compelling storytelling and structured delivery.
